What if you could start a new society on a tiny island in the middle of the ocean? What would you keep, what would you discard, what new ways of life might emerge?

The Travelling Embassy of Rockall is collecting ideas towards a new society - and we want your help.

Rockall is tiny, uninhabited island in the middle of the north Atlantic ocean. Several people have tried and failed to settle there.

Read more about the island.

In summer 2016, the Embassy of Rockall will visit Reykjavík, Iceland.

We’ll host events, installations, workshops and discussions. There’ll be live music, a library, exhibitions and expeditions. Spaces to meet and collaborate, and spaces to relax and play. We’ll build a scale model of the island, we’ll grow food and talk politics, philosophy and economics - and much more besides. Along the way, we hope to discover the ideas and the technology we need to start a new society on Rockall.

You'll find us at Vesturbugt, in the old harbour, from July 1st to September 30th.
